Pasta Salad

This easy pasta salad recipe come together quickly with convenient ingredients and colorful vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of tri-color pasta
  • Bottle of Italian-style dressing
  • Salad seasoning mix
  • Vegetables: Cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and black olives

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss with the seasoned dressing.
