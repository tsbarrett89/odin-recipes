Pesto Pasta

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite pasta
  • 1/2 onion
  • pesto sauce
  • salt and pepper to tast
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with as much cheese as you like.
