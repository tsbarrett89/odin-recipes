Simple Beef Stroganoff

This iconic Russian dish consists of beef in a creamy sauce. This dish is often made with mushrooms and served over noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8oz.) package of egg noodles
  • 1lb. ground beef
  • 1 (10.5oz.) can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Saute ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
